## What Speaker Hub is

Speaker Hub is a web app (SaaS) that gives professional and aspiring public
speakers every tool they need in one place. A speaker uploads a recording or
transcript of a talk and gets an AI engagement map: a score plus the exact
moments the audience leaned in and the moments attention slipped. Around that
core analysis sits a set of tools to write scripts, manage bookings, cut
shareable clips, find stages to speak on, and keep everything styled to the
speaker's own brand.

It is a product, not a service business. There is no agency, no speaker bureau,
and no marketplace of speakers to hire. People use it themselves, on the web.

- Canonical URL: https://speaker-hub.com
- Category: business application / SaaS for public speaking
- Platform: web (works in any modern browser)
- Contact: support@speaker-hub.com

## Who it is for

Professional and aspiring public speakers: keynote speakers, speaking coaches,
founders who present, and anyone who wants to get measurably better on stage and
run the business side of speaking from one place. No editing or technical skill
is needed — if you can send an email, you can use it.

## The tools

Free tools (included on every plan):

- **Speech Analyzer** — Upload a talk and get an AI engagement map showing where
  the room leaned in and where it drifted, with specific notes to act on.
- **Talk Library** — Every talk you have analyzed, organized and ready to revisit.
- **Compare** — Put two talks side by side and see what actually moved the room.
- **SEO & AEO** — Drop in your website and get tips to rank on Google and get
  cited by AI answer engines.
- **Coaching Tips** — A fresh, practical coaching tip every week that you can
  schedule and check off.
- **Brand Kit** — Store your colors, logo, fonts, and voice so the whole hub
  stays unmistakably yours.

Core Premium tools ($19/mo):

- **Script Studio** — Draft, sharpen, and rehearse your keynote with an editor
  that helps it sound like you.
- **Talk Editor** — Cut highlight reels and share-ready clips straight from your
  recorded talks, no separate video editor needed.
- **Booking Inbox** — Track every speaking request from first hello to a
  confirmed gig on your calendar, in one pipeline. Includes a public one-sheet.
- **Keynote Description Tailoring** — Store your keynote once, then spin up
  industry-specific versions that keep your voice and core idea.
- **Stage Finder** — Name speakers you admire, find events they have spoken at,
  and get a draft pitch to reach out to organizers.
- **Content Ideas** — Get 20–30 blog, video, and short-form titles that people
  actually search for, written in your brand voice.

Full Premium tools ($49/mo):

- **AI Assistant** — A personal agent that knows your talks, drafts your emails,
  and connects your calendar and inbox.
- **ClipFlow** — Drop in a video or channel, auto-cut social-ready clips, and
  post them straight to your platforms.

## How it works

1. **Upload your talk.** Drop in a recording or paste a transcript. Speaker Hub
   transcribes and reads it in minutes.
2. **See your engagement map.** Get a score plus the exact moments the room
   leaned in and the ones where attention slipped.
3. **Ship your best work.** Tighten the weak spots, cut clips to share, and walk
   on stage knowing the talk is ready.

## FAQ

**Who is Speaker Hub for?**
Professional and aspiring public speakers — keynoters, coaches, founders, and
anyone who wants to get measurably better on stage and run the business side of
speaking from one place.

**Do I need any editing or technical skills?**
No. Upload a talk and the analysis, script tools, and clip editor do the heavy
lifting. If you can send an email, you can use Speaker Hub.

**Is there a free plan?**
Yes. The Free plan lets you analyze your talks with no limit, keep them in your
Talk Library, and compare two talks — no credit card required.

**How does the AI analysis work?**
Your talk gets transcribed, then scored moment by moment for engagement. You see
exactly where attention rose and fell, with specific notes you can act on before
your next performance.

**Can I cancel anytime?**
Yes. Plans are month to month — upgrade, downgrade, or cancel whenever you like
from your account settings.
